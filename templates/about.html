About

This is a Flask application to calculate air properties.

INPUTS:

  • Temperatures must be -40F to 560F
  • Pressure must be 0 psia to 7000 psia
  • Absolute humidity must be 0 gr/lb to 200 gr/lb

 

OUTPUTS:

  • Density - lb/ft^3
  • Saturation Pressure - psia
  • Absolute humidity - lb/lb
  • Relative humidity - %

*Water properties data is sourced from Cengel & Boles: Thermodynamics : An Engineering Approach.
